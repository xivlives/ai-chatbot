Return and Cancellation Policy
Return Policy:
Eligibility: Products can be returned within 30 days of delivery. The items must be unused,
unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags intact.
Process:
1. Initiate a return request through the ShopEase website or customer service.
2. A return label will be provided, and the product should be packed securely.
3. Once the returned item is received and inspected, a refund or exchange will be
processed within 7-10 business days.
Cancellation Policy:
Order Cancellation:
1. Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement for a full refund.
2. If the order has already been shipped, cancellation is not possible. The product can
be returned following the return policy.

Payment and Delivery Policy
Payment Policy:
Payment Methods:
1. Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express.
2. Digital Wallets: PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay.
3. Bank Transfers: Available for select regions.
4. Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for specific locations.
Security: All transactions are encrypted using SSL technology to ensure the safety and
security of customer information.
Delivery Policy:
Delivery Time:
1. Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
2. Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (additional charges apply).
3. Same-Day Delivery: Available in select locations (order before 12 PM).
Shipping Charges:
1. Free shipping on orders above $50.
2. A flat rate of $5 for orders below $50.
Tracking: Customers will receive a tracking number once the order has been shipped,
allowing them to track their package in real-time.
International Shipping: Available with varying delivery times and charges depending on the
destination.
